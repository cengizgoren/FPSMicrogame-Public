KEYBOARD  
  
UP: w or up  
DOWN: s or down  
LEFT: a or left  
RIGHT: d or right  
JUMP: space  
SHOOT:  left mouse  
AIM:  right mouse  
SWITCH WEAPON:  q e or numbers  
CROUCH:  c  
SPRINT:  shift  
JETPACK:  press and hold space  
PAUSE:  tab or p
