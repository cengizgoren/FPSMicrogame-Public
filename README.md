Keyboard  
  
UP:  W OR UP  
DOWN:  S OR DOWN  
LEFT:  A OR LEFT  
RIGHT:  D OR RIGHT  
SHOOT:  LEFT MOUSE BUTTON  
AIM:  RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON  
SWITCH WEAPON:  Q E OR NUMBERS  
JUMP:  SPACE  
CROUCH:  C  
SPRINT:  SHIFT  
JETPACK:  PRESS AND HOLD SPACE  
PAUSE:  TAB OR P
